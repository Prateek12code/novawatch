Who We Are?

NOVA, a burgeoning startup hailing from India, stands as a testament to innovation and entrepreneurial spirit. Founded by the visionary 11-year-old Prateek Shukla, this remarkable company specializes in crafting cutting-edge smartwatches. Despite its modest beginnings, NOVA has swiftly become a noteworthy player in the tech industry. The young founder's passion for technology and a keen understanding of market trends have propelled NOVA to a valuation of 50 crore rupees, showcasing the company's potential to disrupt the market. With a commitment to excellence and a drive for constant innovation, NOVA is poised to make significant strides in the world of smart technology.

At NOVA, our ethos is simple, minimal, and futuristic design—just what you need in a smartwatch.
